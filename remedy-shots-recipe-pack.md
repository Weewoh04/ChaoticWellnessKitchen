# Remedy-Style Shots Recipe Pack for Chaotic Wellness Kitchen

Use this file as source content for adding remedy-style shot recipes to the `recipes.ts` data file in your Next.js project.

## 1) Ginger Lemon Shot

**Best for:** morning reset, quick pick-me-up

**Ingredients:**
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp honey
- 2 tbsp warm water

**Instructions:**
1. Mix all ingredients together.
2. Stir well.
3. Take as a small shot.

**Notes:**
- Bold, bright, and quick.

## 2) Turmeric Honey Shot

**Best for:** cozy mornings, simple ritual

**Ingredients:**
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp honey
- 2 tbsp warm water

**Instructions:**
1. Mix until smooth.
2. Drink immediately.

**Notes:**
- Slightly earthy and warm.
