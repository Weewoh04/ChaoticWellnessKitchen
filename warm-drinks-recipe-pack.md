# Warm Drinks Recipe Pack for Chaotic Wellness Kitchen

Use this file as source content for adding warm drink recipes to the `recipes.ts` data file in your Next.js project.

## 1) Honey Lemon Ginger Hot Water

**Best for:** cozy mornings, scratchy throat days, comfort hydration

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup hot water
- 1 to 2 tsp honey
- 1/2 lemon, juiced
- 4 to 6 slices fresh ginger

**Instructions:**
1. Heat water until hot.
2. Add ginger and steep 5 to 8 minutes.
3. Stir in lemon juice and honey.
4. Sip warm.

**Notes:**
- Cozy, simple, and grounding.

## 2) Golden Turmeric Milk

**Best for:** evening wind-down, cozy nights

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup milk (any kind)
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp honey
- pinch of cinnamon

**Instructions:**
1. Heat milk gently.
2. Stir in turmeric and cinnamon.
3. Add honey and mix well.
4. Serve warm.

**Notes:**
- Warm and slightly spiced.

---

## 3) Cinnamon Honey Sleep Tea

**Best for:** bedtime routine, calming evenings

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup hot water
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 1 tsp honey

**Instructions:**
1. Add cinnamon stick to hot water.
2. Steep 5 to 10 minutes.
3. Stir in honey.
4. Drink warm.

**Notes:**
- Simple and comforting.

## 4) Clove Orange Tea

## 8) Salted Honey Hydration Tea

**Best for:** gentle hydration, reset days

- 1 cup warm water
