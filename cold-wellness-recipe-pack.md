# Cold Wellness & Hydration Recipe Pack for Chaotic Wellness Kitchen

Use this file as source content for adding cold wellness recipes to the `recipes.ts` data file in your Next.js project.

## 1) Cucumber Mint Calm Water

**Best for:** hydration, warm afternoons, light refresh

**Ingredients:**
- 4 cups cold water
- 6 cucumber slices
- 6 mint leaves
- Ice (optional)

**Instructions:**
1. Add cucumber and mint to a pitcher.
2. Pour in cold water.
3. Chill for at least 20 minutes.
4. Serve cold.

**Notes:**
- Light and refreshing.
- Best enjoyed same day.

---

## 2) Lemon Ginger Ice Cubes

**Best for:** quick wellness drinks, easy add-ins

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup water
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 2 tbsp lemon juice

**Instructions:**
1. Mix all ingredients.
2. Pour into ice cube tray.
3. Freeze until solid.
4. Add cubes to water or tea.

**Notes:**
- Great for fast flavor without prep.

---

## 3) Strawberry Basil Hydration Water

**Best for:** summer days, light fruity drinks

**Ingredients:**
- 4 cups cold water
- 3 strawberries, sliced
- 4 basil leaves

**Instructions:**
1. Add strawberries and basil to water.
2. Chill for 20–30 minutes.
3. Serve cold.

**Notes:**
- Slightly sweet and fresh.

---

## 4) Orange Salt Electrolyte Water

**Best for:** hydration, reset days

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup water
- juice of 1/2 orange
- pinch of salt
- 1 tsp honey (optional)

**Instructions:**
1. Mix all ingredients.
2. Stir until combined.
3. Drink chilled or room temp.

**Notes:**
- Simple DIY electrolyte-style drink.

---

## 5) Chia Fresca Energy Drink

**Best for:** light energy, hydration

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup water
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tbsp lemon or lime juice
- 1 tsp honey

**Instructions:**
1. Mix all ingredients.
2. Let sit 5–10 minutes until chia softens.
3. Stir and drink.

**Notes:**
- Slight texture from chia.

---

## 6) Frozen Herbal Tea Cubes

**Best for:** quick iced tea, flavor boosters

**Ingredients:**
- Brewed herbal tea (cooled)

**Instructions:**
1. Pour cooled tea into ice cube tray.
2. Freeze until solid.
3. Add to water for flavor.

**Notes:**
- Works with any tea type.

---

## 7) Watermelon Lime Refresher

**Best for:** hot days, refreshing drinks

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup watermelon chunks
- juice of 1/2 lime
- 1/2 cup cold water

**Instructions:**
1. Blend all ingredients.
2. Strain if desired.
3. Serve cold.

**Notes:**
- Naturally sweet and hydrating.
