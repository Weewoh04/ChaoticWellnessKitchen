# Cozy Add-Ons Recipe Pack for Chaotic Wellness Kitchen

Use this file as source content for adding cozy add-on recipes to the `recipes.ts` data file in your Next.js project.

## 1) Honey Butter Toast

**Best for:** sick days, quick comfort, simple meals

**Ingredients:**
- 1 slice bread
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp honey

**Instructions:**
1. Toast the bread.
2. Spread butter while warm.
3. Drizzle honey on top.
4. Serve immediately.

**Notes:**
- Simple, cozy, and comforting.

---

## 2) Garlic Herb Toast

**Best for:** pairing with broths and soups

**Ingredients:**
- 1 slice bread
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- pinch of dried herbs

**Instructions:**
1. Mix butter, garlic, and herbs.
2. Spread on bread.
3. Toast until golden.
4. Serve warm.

**Notes:**
- Perfect side for broth recipes.

---

## 3) Soft Scrambled Eggs

**Best for:** light meals, recovery days

**Ingredients:**
- 2 eggs
- 1 tbsp butter
- pinch of salt

**Instructions:**
1. Melt butter in pan over low heat.
2. Add eggs and stir slowly.
3. Cook until soft and creamy.
4. Season and serve.

**Notes:**
- Keep heat low for best texture.

---

## 4) Simple Rice Bowl

**Best for:** easy digestion, simple meals

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup cooked rice
- pinch of salt
- optional: drizzle of butter or broth

**Instructions:**
1. Prepare rice.
2. Add salt and optional butter or broth.
3. Serve warm.

**Notes:**
- Very gentle and filling.

---

## 5) Herb-Infused Honey

**Best for:** adding to teas and drinks

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup honey
- fresh herbs (mint, thyme, or rosemary)

**Instructions:**
1. Place herbs in jar.
2. Pour honey over herbs.
3. Let sit for a few days.
4. Use as needed.

**Notes:**
- Store at room temperature.

---

## 6) Cinnamon Sugar Warm Oats

**Best for:** cozy mornings

**Ingredients:**
- 1/2 cup oats
- 1 cup water or milk
- 1 tsp sugar
- pinch cinnamon

**Instructions:**
1. Cook oats with liquid.
2. Stir in sugar and cinnamon.
3. Serve warm.

**Notes:**
- Simple and comforting breakfast.

---

## 7) Buttered Crackers & Broth Plate

**Best for:** low energy meals, comfort days

**Ingredients:**
- crackers
- butter
- warm broth

**Instructions:**
1. Spread butter on crackers.
2. Serve alongside warm broth.
3. Eat together.

**Notes:**
- Minimal effort comfort meal.
