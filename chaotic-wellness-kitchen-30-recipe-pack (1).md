# Cozy Broths

## 1) Sick Day Sip Broth
**Best for:** sick days, low energy, no appetite, cozy recovery

**Ingredients:**
- 2 to 3 cups chicken broth or veggie broth
- 1 small clove garlic, lightly smashed (optional)
- pinch of salt, if needed
- squeeze of lemon (optional)
- pinch of turmeric (optional)

**Instructions:**
1. Pour broth into a small pot.
2. Add garlic and turmeric if using.
3. Heat gently until warm, not aggressively boiling.
4. Remove garlic if desired.
5. Add lemon if using.
6. Pour into a mug and sip slowly.

**Notes:**
- Meant to be sipped, not eaten like a full soup.
- Many people enjoy this when they want something warm but very light.

## 2) Golden Chicken Bone Broth Soup
**Best for:** cozy reset days, feeling run down, comfort meals

**Ingredients:**
- 6 cups chicken bone broth
- 1 to 2 cups shredded chicken
- 2 carrots, sliced
- 2 celery stalks, chopped
- 1/2 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- salt to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Bring broth to a gentle simmer.
2. Add carrots, celery, onion, and garlic.
3. Simmer 15 to 20 minutes.
4. Stir in turmeric and chicken.
5. Simmer 5 to 10 minutes more.
6. Salt to taste and serve warm.

**Notes:**
- Add noodles or rice if desired.
- Cozy and grounding.

---

## 3) Garlic Onion Comfort Broth
**Best for:** simple comfort, light meals, low appetite days

**Ingredients:**
- 4 cups broth
- 4 cloves garlic, smashed
- 1/2 onion, sliced
- 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
- salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Heat oil in a pot.
2. Sauté garlic and onion until softened.
3. Add broth and simmer 10 to 15 minutes.
4. Season and serve warm.

**Notes:**
- Serve with toast or crackers.
- Can be strained for a smoother broth.
